# Hudson Group Business Divisions & Products

## 1. Mold Business (模具事业)
**Focus**: Difficult-to-construct, high-precision molds.

### Capabilities
- **HMMS MES**: Mold Manufacturing Management System for tracking and efficiency.
- **Semi-Automated Manufacturing**: Advanced layout and functional features for efficiency.
- **3D Metal Printing**: Used for precision mold applications, especially for cooling channels (conformal cooling) to reduce cycle time and deformation.
    - *Example*: Reduced cycle time from >70s to 55s by eliminating hot spots.
    - *Example*: Improved cooling uniformity (temp difference reduced from 7.3°C to 2.3°C), reducing part deformation.

### Key Products
- **Ink System Bracket**: 
    - Office equipment category.
    - Material: PPE Z552H BLACK.
    - Challenges: Strict tolerances (+/- 0.05mm for 75% of dims), complex inserts, extensive cooling requirements.
- Various automotive and office automation precision molds.

## 2. Injection Business (注塑事业)
**Focus**: Low-cost, high-quality parts.

### Capabilities
- **Injection MES**: Management system for injection molding.
- **Equipment**: Modern injection molding machines.
- **Products**:
    - **Appearance Parts**: High-finish aesthetic components.
    - **Functional Parts**: Precision internal mechanisms.

## 3. Assembly Business (组装事业)
**Focus**: Aspiring to be the Industry No. 1 Assembly Factory.

### Facilities
- **Shenzhen Assembly Line**: Located at HQ.
- **Thailand Factory 1 & 2**: Expansion into Southeast Asia.
- **Vietnam Assembly Line**: Further regional coverage.

### Capabilities
- Comprehensive assembly services ("One-stop" from mold to assembly).
- Automated assembly projects.

## 4. Automation Business (自动化事业)
**Focus**: Integration of labor-saving technical solutions.

### Services
- **Measurement Jigs**: Development of precision measurement tools.
- **Automation Jigs**: Custom jigs for automated production.
- **Shenzhen Automation Examples**: Case studies of implemented automation lines.
- **Goal**: Commercialization of high value-added products through automation.

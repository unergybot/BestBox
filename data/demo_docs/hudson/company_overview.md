# Hudson Group Company Overview

## General Information
**Hudson Group** (listing entities like Hudson Industrial Development (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd., Shenzhen Kaicheng Precision Mold Co., Ltd., and Shenzhen Hudson Smart Equipment Co., Ltd.) is an integrated manufacturing group located in Shenzhen, China.

- **Mission**: To provide high-quality, comprehensive services by integrating mold design/manufacturing, injection molding, automated labor-saving solutions, and assembly production.
- **Vision**: To build robust business groups delivering high value-added products.

## Subsidiaries & Locations

- **Established**: November 2007
- **Address**: Darxun Industrial Park, Pingxin North Road, Shangmugu, Pinghu, Longgang District, Shenzhen
- **Investment**: 8.8 Million RMB
- **Employees**: ~371
- **Facility Size**: 12,250 m²
- **Currencies**: USD / HKD (Import/Export/Transfer)

## Core Competence
Hudson builds a strong business group by integrating four key pillars:
1. **Mold Business**: Constructing high-precision molds.
2. **Injection Business**: Producing low-cost, high-quality component parts.
3. **Assembly Business**: Striving to be the industry's No. 1 assembly factory.
4. **Automation Business**: Integrating automated and labor-saving technical solutions to commercialize high value-added products.
